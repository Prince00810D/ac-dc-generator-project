AC To DC Generator

Design Project Presentation

Introduction

AC generators produce alternating current while DC generators produce direct current using commutators.

Aim

To convert AC current into DC current using rectifier circuits.

Objectives

  • Understand AC generator
  • Convert AC to DC
  • Study electrical components
  • Generate electricity mechanically

Working

Mechanical energy rotates a coil in magnetic field producing AC current which is converted to DC.

Applications

  • Battery Charging
  • Industrial Motors
  • Electronics
  • Power Systems
